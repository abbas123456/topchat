Getting started

Follow this simple tutorial to add a chat room to your website. Once you're happy go ahead and register.

  • Extra steps

1. Register

The easiest step, type in a username and password and click on "Register".

2. Create room

After registration you will be taken to the dashboard page. This page lets you manage all your chat rooms. Since we have no chat rooms yet click on "New room".

Fill in the details for your chat room and click on "Create room"

3. Add to your website

To add the chat room to your website go to the "Your website" section of the dashboard and copy the html code supplied.

You need to then paste the html code onto your website on the page you want the chat room to appear. You can control the height and width of the chat room by changing the height and width pixel values in the html code.

4. Customise room

After adding the chat room to your website you may want to customise it.. After creating your chat room you will be taken back to the dashboard page. To customise the appearance of the chat room click on "Appearance".

You will now see a preview of your chat room with the default colour scheme.

To change the background or text colour, click on the appropriate field and choose a colour from the colour picker. As you change the colour the preview will be automatically updated.

Once you are happy with the colours click on "Save".

5. Room admins

To manage your chat room administrators click on "Administrators", you will see that your username has already been added to the list of administrators as you created the chat room.

To add another administrator search for their username in the empty search box and click "Save".

To delete an adminsitrator simply click on the trash icon next to their name.

To manage users that are banned from the chat room, follow the same instructions as above in the "User management" section of the dashboard.
